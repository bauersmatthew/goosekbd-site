Classy. Cool.
But not too complicated.

The lineage of Mechanical Keyboards has been characterized by success after success after success. There've been so many incredible boards strut their way into the limelight that it's hard to know where to even start when looking for ways to improve them.

But, when we examined the plethora of Pok3rs, Plancks, and Preonics, we found one thing they were all missing: the goose factor.

The goose factor is this inexplicable, inconprehensible feature to a keyboard that just makes it better. Of course, we didn't just stumble upon this factor immediately; it took us at Goose KBD years of grueling, diligent trial and error just to bear priviledged witness to the outline of its glory.

Finally, at long last, we present to you:

The G01.

With this delectable LED-strip-adorned 60% keyboard kit with a case of solid tungsten, we hope you find that perfect blend of classiness, playfulness, and unashamed charm that we know we've always been looking for.

It's not out just yet, but just two short months from now in February 2017 we plan to bring the G01 to loyal customers all throughout the world. However, we plan to push out constant updates and progress reports to the place where this whole thing started: /r/mechanicalkeyboards.

Of course, this monumental task would never be possible alone. Last but not least, we'd like to thank the good people at mechkeys.ca for their continued support with this project, making absolutely sure that it lives up to its fullest possible potential.
